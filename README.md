# Moshed — Your Concert Diary

Every show. Every memory. Built by a music fan, for music fans.

## Features

### Logging Shows
- Add concerts manually or import from Mail, Photos, and Apple Wallet
- Calendar scan — bulk import from Ticketmaster, Dice, AXS and more
- Share Extension — share a ticket email or screenshot directly to Moshed
- Duplicate detection — Moshed won't create the same show twice

### Your Diary
- Rate shows, write notes, and log the setlist
- Track spending per show and across your history
- Add companions — see who you've been to the most shows with
- Photo gallery per concert

### Stats & Insights
- Total shows, venues, artists, and spend
- Top artists, venues, and years
- Year in Review — your concert highlights at a glance
- Genre breakdown and listening habits

### Festival Planner
- Build a full festival schedule by day and stage
- Mark sets as Attending, Not Going, or Undecided
- Conflict detection for overlapping sets
- Sets you attend become full diary entries automatically

### Premium (Moshed+)
- Unlimited photos per show
- Advanced stats and Year in Review
- Artist genre backfill
- Export to CSV and PDF
- 7-day free trial

## Privacy

Moshed does not collect any personal data. All your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see, access, or share your information.
