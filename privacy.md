# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

Moshed ("the app") is a personal concert diary. Your data stays on your device.

## Data We Collect

**We collect nothing.** Moshed does not have servers, accounts, or analytics. All data you enter — shows, photos, notes, ratings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and backed up only to your personal iCloud (if you have iCloud backup enabled).

## Third-Party Services

Moshed makes outbound requests to these public APIs to look up artist information. Only artist names are sent — no personal data.

| Service | Purpose | Privacy |
|---|---|---|
| [Deezer](https://www.deezer.com/legal/personal-datas) | Artist photos | No account required |
| [MusicBrainz](https://metabrainz.org/privacy) | Artist metadata | No account required |
| [iTunes Search API](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) | Track and release info | No account required |

## Calendar & Contacts Access

If you grant calendar access, Moshed reads event titles and dates to find past concerts. This data never leaves your device. Contacts access is optional and used only to tag companions on shows you log.

## Subscriptions (Moshed+)

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. Moshed never sees your payment details. See [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for how subscription data is handled.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data, delete the app. Nothing is stored outside your device or iCloud account.
